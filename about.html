ABOUT-INTRODUCTION

SAYLANI WELFARRE INTENATIONAL TRUST , in May 1999 by spiritual scholar MAULANA BASHIR FAROOQI QADRI is a prominent non-governmental organization (NGO)

                                    , dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged individuals, especially in Pakistan. Driven by a mission to break the cycle of poverty and spread happiness, Saylani has grown exponentially over its 25-year history, extending its physical presence across Pakistan and establishing overseas offices in the UK, USA, and UAE. The trust operates across more than 60 sectors, providing a comprehensive range of free services that include food, education, medical care, and social welfare. Its vast network of 125 branches, supported by a dedicated team of over 2,000 professionals, serves approximately 125,000 people daily, demonstrating a remarkable scale of humanitarian endeavor. Through its diverse programs, Saylani offers crucial support to vulnerable communities, from feeding thousands daily through its Dastarkhwan (meal center) initiative to empowering youth with modern technological skills via the Saylani Mass IT Training (SMIT) program. It has been instrumental in providing economic empowerment by helping the unemployed find jobs and assisting with essential equipment like rickshaws and sewing machines. Saylani also addresses critical health needs through specialized medical facilities, such as the Saylani Chest Care Center and Diabetic Center, alongside a Thalassemia & Blood Bank Centre. Beyond its work in Pakistan, the organization has consistently responded to international crises, including providing aid to refugees from Syria and Rohingya, as well as delivering emergency relief during natural disasters and global events like the COVID-19 pandemic. With a strong reputation for transparency and efficiency, Saylani ensures that donor contributions, which are the backbone of its operations, have a measurable and lasting impact on the communities it serves.

vision

Saylani Welfare's vision is to create a compassionate world where every individual, from birth to old age, has access to basic necessities, opportunities for growth, and the dignity of self-reliance.

MISSION

Saylani Welfare's mission is to serve humanity across every stage of life by alleviating suffering, nurturing self-sufficiency, and bringing hope and dignity to millions worldwide through initiatives in food, healthcare, education, and economic empowerment.
